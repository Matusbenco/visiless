Partnership FAQ

Everything you need to know before your first order

What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Our goal is to support smaller shops as well. The minimum value of the first order is set very accessibly so that adding a new brand doesn't represent a financial burden. You will find specific numbers in our wholesale price list.

Do you provide materials for e-shops?

Yes, immediately after establishing a partnership, we will send you access to an XML/CSV feed, a folder with professional cutout photos of the products, and optimized descriptions ready to import into your system.

Is it possible to exchange unsold assortment?

We understand that every fishing ground and customer is different. If you find that a certain length or variant isn't selling as expected, we will gladly exchange the goods for more requested variants without any problems.

Can I try the product before selling it?

Absolutely. We know an angler needs to touch the product. Write to us via the form below, and we will gladly arrange the delivery of test samples or provide you with more detailed product information.
