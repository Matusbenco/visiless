Story and Vision

VISILESS® Born right by the water - through honest practice

We are a team of professional anglers and nature lovers who refused to make compromises. Our rigs are the result of decades of testing in clear water fisheries. Today, we bring this proven product to the B2B network to help specialized stores offer their customers quality they can rely on.

"We manufacture every rig as if every cast meant a bite from a trophy catch. We pass this certainty on to you and your customers."

FAQ about cooperation

Everything you need to know before your first order

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our goal is to support even smaller stores. The minimum value of the first order is set very affordably, so adding a new brand will not be a financial burden for you. Specific numbers can be found in our price list.

Do you provide materials for e-shops?

Yes, immediately after establishing cooperation, we will send you access to an XML/CSV feed, a folder with professional cutout photos of products, and optimized descriptions ready for import into your system.

Is it possible to exchange unsuitable assortment?

We understand that every fishery and customer is different. If you find out that a certain length or test weight doesn't sell as expected, we will easily exchange the goods for more requested variants.

Is it possible to try the product before selling?

Absolutely. We know an angler needs to feel the product. Write to us via the form below and we will gladly arrange the delivery of test samples or provide you with more detailed information about the products.
